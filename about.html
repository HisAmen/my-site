A Citizen of Lithuania

My name is Nojus. I am a citizen of Lithuania. I always believed in God, but that didn't mean I knew Him. I was never appealed to Christianity because I didn't like how the Catholic Church portrayed Christianity.

Then, at fourteen years old, something happened at school. I opened a Bible. Not because anyone asked me to. Not because I was searching for anything specific. I was just curious.

The rest is history. Shortly after that I became a Christian and don't know how myself. It's all Jesus! Not because of tradition. Not because of family pressure. Because I encountered something true, and I could not walk away from it.

📖 Why This Archive Exists

Over the years, I noticed something frustrating. Many people — good, honest people — do not believe in God. But almost none of them have seen the actual evidence. They have heard arguments. They have heard slogans. They have not been shown the data.

So I started collecting. Every time I found a peer-reviewed study, a documented miracle, a prophecy fulfilled, a scientific anomaly that defies natural explanation — I saved it. This archive is the result.

I do not ask anyone to take my word for it. I do not ask for blind faith. I ask only for honest examination. Look at what is actually documented. Look at the Shroud of Turin. Look at the Lourdes medical files. Look at the odds Peter Stoner calculated for just eight prophecies. Look at the fine-tuning numbers from Barrow and Tipler.

Only then decide...

🎯 The Goal

People should know the reasons for belief. Not just "I feel like God is real" — but actual, verifiable reasons. Evidence that can be checked. Sources that can be traced.

This archive is for the skeptic who is willing to look. For the Christian who wants to strengthen their faith. For the curious who just want to know if any of this is real.

I believe God is real. Not because I was raised that way. Because I looked at the evidence, and the evidence pointed to Him. Besides, God can be perfectly known apart from all these arguments, because He exists.

— Nojus (hisforeveramen on Discord)
